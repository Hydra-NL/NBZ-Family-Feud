NBZ Family Feud

Rules & Instructions

Question Rounds

At the start of each round a face off question gets asked. One player of both teams (usually the team leader), will face off against each other. The host will ask a question and if the player thinks they know an answer they press their buzzer/bell as fast as they can. The first player to press their buzzer/bell first, gets to answer first. The player with the highest score answer on the board, gets to choose if their team will play or pass this question.

The goal of choosing to play a question, is to clear the board with each member giving one answer in sequence. Giving an incorrect answer, or failing to respond, earns a STRIKE. If the team gets three(3) STRIKEs before clearing the board, the opposite team now gets the chance to steal the question.

STRIKE

The team can now discuss with each other, once they all agree to an answer the Team leader gives the answer to the host. If the answer given is on the board their team will steal ALL the points earned by the opposite team in this round. If an incorrect answer is given, the team that was originally in control of the question keeps their points.

If the team chooses to pass the question, the other team gets to go first in an attempt to clear the board.

Bonus Rounds

The team leader of the winning team, will go first in answering five questions within 25 seconds.

The team leader of the losing team does not get to hear the given answers as they are going to try to answer the same questions next. However they will get 30 seconds to answer the same five questions, duplicate answers are not allowed.

After both team leaders have given their answers they will be revealed to everyone and both teams will receive the points accordingly.

Points

Points are based on the amount of people who gave the same reponse to the survey.

[EXAMPLE]: 'Name a red fruit'.
64/100 people responded with 'Apple', the team that guesses 'Apple' will receive 64 points.

Round Multipliers
Round 1: 1x
Round 2: 2x
Round 3: 3x
